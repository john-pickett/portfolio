Stats Hound

Synopsis

Everyone with a blog or website wants to know how many people are visiting and reading their content.

Stats Hound makes it easy to track the visits and traffic your website is getting every day. It can even track sales, new users, and more.

Stats Hound was built in Rails.

Charlotte Mason Community

Synopsis

Charlotte Mason was an educator in Britain in the late 1800s and early 1900s. Today her teaching philosophy and instructional methods have inspired a traditional, literature-based home school curriculum.

The Charlotte Mason Community website was created to provide home-schooling parents an easy way to share resources, compare notes, and meet other home-schooling families.

Bloc Chat

Synopsis

Bloc Chat makes it easy and fun to discuss your favorite topics with other fans. Users can create a user name, join an existing chat room, or create a room to discuss a new topic.

Bloc Chat was written in Angular, and is hooked up to Firebase for the back end.

Blocipedia

Synopsis

A simplified version of Wikipedia, Blocipedia lets users set up accounts, and create and edit wikis on any topic they choose.

To keep the spirit of Wikipedia, any user is allowed to edit any public wiki. However, users can purchase a premium account to create private wikis, and give access to collaborators if they wish.
